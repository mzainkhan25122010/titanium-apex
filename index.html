Premier Engineering Competition

Formula Pakistan is a Minature Formula Car Racing competition for 14-19 year old enthusiasts who will be designing and racing their miniature version of F1 style cars on the competition day. Students use sophisticated CAD/CAM softwares to design the cars and then using CNC Machining and 3D Printing they bring the vehicle to life. Principles of aerodynamics are applied to make it go faster, the competition is based on the fastest car winning.

Formula Pakistan is not just a competition; it is a journey that exposes students to role models, healthy competition, and self-driven growth across multiple stages. By designing, building, and racing miniature Formula-style cars, students learn to lead, create, and drive real-world outcomes, fostering a culture of innovation and continuous improvement.

Competition Focus Areas

Teams are evaluated across multiple disciplines:

  • Engineering Design & Innovation
  • Cost Analysis & Manufacturing Planning
  • Business Case Presentation
  • On-Track Dynamic Events
  • Overall Vehicle Performance & Reliability

This holistic approach ensures that participants develop not only technical skills but also business acumen and professional competencies.

Our Formula Pakistan Journey

Team Formation & Vision

Six engineering students with diverse backgrounds came together with a shared passion for motorsport and innovation. Established team structure, defined goals, and laid the foundation for TITANIUM APEX.

Design & Development Phase

Months of intensive design work using advanced CAD software, CFD simulations, and finite element analysis. Faced challenges in balancing performance, weight, and cost constraints while maintaining innovation.

Manufacturing Challenges

Sourcing materials, learning composite fabrication techniques, and overcoming manufacturing limitations. Developed practical solutions through iterative prototyping and testing.

First Competition Experience

Debuted at Formula Pakistan 2022 with a functional prototype. Faced technical difficulties during dynamic events but gained invaluable real-world testing experience and team bonding.

Technical Refinements

Applied lessons learned to redesign critical components, improve reliability, and enhance performance. Implemented a rigorous testing protocol and data analysis system.

Current Season & Future Goals

Competing with a significantly improved vehicle featuring innovative aerodynamic solutions and optimized powertrain. Aiming for top positions in design and dynamic events while expanding our technical capabilities.
